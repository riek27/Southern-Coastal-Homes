ABOUT US

Southern Coastal Homes

Southern Coastal Homes is an award-winning, custom builder with a rich history in the building industry. We are a smaller-volume boutique builder focused purely on residential construction with a strong emphasis on personal attention and expert craftsmanship. Both of the business owners have over 25 years in the building industry, are extremely involved in each unique project, and are genuinely interested in delivering extraordinary results.

Scott Middleton is a CPA who comes from the financial sector of the homebuilding business. He views your custom residence as not only the place you live and enjoy but also as a significant investment in your financial portfolio. His focus is to deliver both enjoyment and fiscal value in a home. Scott works in conjunction with each client to create realistic budgets and structure the monetary planning involved with a custom build.

Anne Middleton is both an operations and design expert from the homebuilding industry. Anne and her team work closely with each client to ensure that form, function, and aesthetics are fused perfectly together to create the ideal custom home. Gone are the old days of the in-office showrooms – Anne works in partnership with you and true field experts to ensure you are selecting from the most innovative home design products available on the market.

From our company owners to the marketing, operations, and project management, the Southern Coastal Homes team is fully committed to utilizing the highest quality of materials and building practices, while only working with carefully curated subcontractors and master craftsmen. Our innovative approach to home building has definitively set us apart from the competition.

Let your dreams become our next project.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ ABOUT THE HOME BUILDING PROCESS

HOW DO I KNOW SOUTHERN COASTAL HOMES IS THE RIGHT BUILDER FOR ME?

At Southern Coastal Homes we truly believe that building your dream home is a collaborative effort. Your home should be a reflection of you and the things you love. It should be thoughtfully designed to reflect the way you want to utilize your spaces. We listen closely and help to translate your ideas into your vision.

We pride ourselves on building a spectacular home at a fair and reasonable price and we firmly believe you will find our company fair and transparent. Anyone can build to a plan. It truly gets down to our commitment, mastery of craftsmanship, attention to detail, focus on quality, and quite frankly… trust between the client and the builder.

We encourage you to start a discussion. We would love to help.

WHAT MAKES SOUTHERN COASTAL HOMES UNIQUE?

We are a boutique custom home builder focused on personal attention and expert craftsmanship. Our owners have over 25 years of experience and are directly involved in every project. We combine financial expertise with design excellence to create homes that are both beautiful and valuable investments.

WHAT IS YOUR PRICE PER SQUARE FOOT?

Because we build truly custom homes, our prices vary depending on the complexity of design, materials selected, and site conditions. We provide transparent, detailed estimates for each project after understanding your specific needs and preferences. We focus on delivering value through quality craftsmanship rather than competing on price alone.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO BUILD A NEW HOME?

The timeline for building a custom home typically ranges from 9 to 18 months, depending on the size and complexity of the project, as well as current market conditions. We provide a detailed project timeline during the planning phase and maintain regular communication throughout the process to keep you informed of progress.

I LIVE OUT OF STATE RIGHT NOW, BUT AM MOVING TO THE LOWCOUNTRY. CAN I STILL BUILD A HOME WITH YOU?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with clients who are relocating to the area. We utilize technology for virtual meetings, provide regular photo updates, and ensure clear communication throughout the process. Many of our clients successfully build their dream homes while living out of state.

WHERE DOES SOUTHERN COASTAL HOMES BUILD?

We build throughout the South Carolina Lowcountry, including Bluffton, Hilton Head Island, Beaufort, and surrounding areas. We have experience building in many of the region's premier communities including Colleton River, Wexford, Palmetto Bluff, Belfair, and Port Royal.
